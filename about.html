About WebEd

WebEd is a modern, powerful, and intuitive visual website editor created for designers, developers, and creators who want to build fast and responsive websites — without writing a single line of code.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower designers, developers, and beginners with tools that simplify the web creation process. We aim to deliver a seamless editing experience that reduces complexity and unlocks creativity.

Our story

WebEd started at home, on a mobile phone, while working with hundreds of lines of code. A simple thought came up — what if this code could be built visually, like a website builder? Existing builders were powerful, but they were made mainly for PC users. There was no true solution focused on mobile creators. That gap became the idea behind WebEd. WebEd is a mobile-first website builder, designed to work smoothly on phones. At the same time, it’s fully responsive and works seamlessly on desktop as well. The goal is simple: make website building easy, fast, and accessible on any device. WebEd is built to adapt to creators — not force creators to adapt to tools.

What We Do

We provide a fast, flexible, and intuitive web editing experience packed with smart tools such as drag-and-drop layouts, responsive design controls, template presets, integrated code editing, and project management. Whether you're a beginner or a pro, WebEd helps you build websites your way.

Meet The Founder

WebEd was founded by Sahil with a vision to simplify web design for everyone. With a strong focus on user experience and innovation, Sahil continues to lead WebEd towards creating a more powerful and accessible web editing ecosystem.
